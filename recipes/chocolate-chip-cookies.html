Chocolate chip cookies

Description

chocolate chocolate chip cookies are made with cocoa powder and chocolate chips to guarantee chocolaty flavor in every bite. My kids love them!

Cookie Ingredients

  • Sugar The recipe starts with 1 ½ cups white sugar.
  • Butter Beat the sugar with two sticks of softened butter.
  • EggsTwo eggs lend moisture and act as a binding agent, which means they help hold the dough together.
  • VanillaVanilla extract enhances the overall flavor of the chocolate chocolate chip cookies.
  • FlourAll-purpose flour gives the cookie dough structure.
  • Cocoa powder They wouldnt be chocolate chocolate chip cookies without cocoa powder!
  • Baking sodaBaking soda acts as a leavener, which means it helps the cookies rise.
  • SaltA pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it wont make the cookies taste salty.
  • Chocolate chipsYou’ll need two cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into butter mixture until well blended. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven just until set, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the cookie sheets before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
